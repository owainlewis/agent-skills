# Kit email

Write a Kit email for: $ARGUMENTS

---

## What this is

A short email to your Kit subscriber list pointing them to a new video. Not a sales pitch. Not a mini-article. A pointer.

---

## Structure

```
Hey {first_name},

[2-4 sentences: why this matters, what you built/tested/learned, what the video covers]

You can watch the video here.

[VIDEO PREVIEW]

Talk soon,
Owain
```

---

## Constraints

- Under 100 words (excluding sign-off)
- One link only (the video)
- First person, conversational

---

## Examples

<example>

I've been testing Claude Code's new agent teams feature — where multiple Claude instances work together on the same project.

In my latest video, I build an app from scratch with three agents (backend, frontend, code reviewer) all working in parallel. I also break down when to use agent teams vs subagents.

You can watch the video here.

[VIDEO PREVIEW]

Talk soon,
Owain
</example>

Present the complete email (ready to paste into Kit) and word count.
